My Cat: Lacey

This is my cat Lacey. She is a Maine Coon mix, and I've had her since I was in the 1st grade. This means that this April, she will be 15 years old, which is a very long life span for a cat. Even with how old she is, she still acts like a kitten. She's still very energetic, friendly, and full of love. During middle and high school, whenever I would do homework on my laptop in my bed, she would jump up beside me and lay against me under my arm. I would put my arm over her, and sometimes she'd look back up at me and it had to be the cutest thing ever. I have so many old photos of me as a little kid with her, and we pretty much grew up together. She has a very silly history too, where she's done a lot of dangerous things but somehow survived. At an old apartment, she jumped off a second story balcony and landed in a tree unscathed. She's also escaped and survived in the woods a couple times, returning home like absolutely nothing has happened. I love going home every once in a while to see her, since no matter how long I'm gone, she still loves to be with me.

Lacey's Favorite Foods:

  • Steak
